About Suri’s Legal Consulting

Suri’s Legal Consulting is a professional legal advisory and consulting firm based in Madurai, Tamil Nadu, offering ethical, strategic, and solution-oriented legal services to individuals, startups, businesses, and corporate entities.

Founded under the leadership of Senior Advocate Siva Suriya Narayanan, the firm is built on strong legal fundamentals, professional integrity, and client trust. We focus on simplifying complex legal frameworks and translating them into clear, practical, and compliant solutions.

Our legal consulting practice is guided by integrity, confidentiality, strong legal research, preventive advisory, and risk mitigation. We believe effective legal consulting is proactive and long-term, not merely reactive.

With advisory presence across Madurai, Chennai, Tirunelveli, and Delhi, we serve clients throughout Tamil Nadu and across India, supporting them with compliance-driven and business-oriented legal guidance.

Our Legal Journey & Practice Values

2015 Foundation in Advocacy

The practice originated with strong courtroom advocacy, legal research, and hands-on experience in civil and commercial matters.

2018 Expansion into Legal Consulting

Transitioned into structured legal advisory services focusing on documentation, compliance, and preventive legal strategies for individuals and businesses.

2020 Corporate & Startup Advisory

Dedicated legal consulting for startups, SMEs, and corporate entities covering contracts, governance, and regulatory compliance.

2023 Multi-Location Practice

Strengthened presence across Tamil Nadu and Delhi, serving clients nationwide while maintaining region-specific legal expertise.
